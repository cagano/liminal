# Decisions Log

Cross-exploration record of every deliberate fork. When a future session asks
"why did we do it this way?" — the answer should be here, not buried in a
conversation that got compressed.

---

## Format

| Date | Exploration | Fork | Chosen | Deferred | Reasoning |
|------|------------|------|--------|----------|-----------|
| — | — | — | — | — | — |

---

## Rules

- Every exploration produces at least one decision entry, even if the decision is "defer this entirely."
- If a deferred option gets revisited later, add a new row — don't edit the old one. Decisions are append-only.
- Reference explorations by slug, e.g., `async-brainstorm` links to `.agents/explorations/async-brainstorm.md`.
